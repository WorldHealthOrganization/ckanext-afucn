Overview

The Open Data Portal of the World Health Organization Regional Office for Africa (WHO AFRO) is managed by the BI Team in the Universal Health Coverage, Communicable and Noncommunicable Diseases (UCN) Cluster

The portal provides open access to health data, supporting data-driven strategies to address public health challenges across the African region.
